Rep. Espaillat Appropriations Wins

Every year, as a member of the House Committee on Appropriations, Congressman Espaillat attends hearings on 12 appropriations bills, each setting the yearly budget for a part of the government. And in every hearing, Rep. Espaillat fights to secure funding for our local community and important programs that have been underfunded.

This interactive tool developed by our office allows constituents to explore the funding I have helped secure for my most important legislative priorities.
